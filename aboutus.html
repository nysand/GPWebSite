About Us

The Over Surgery was founded in 2017 and we own seven other healthcare facilities in England. We are a private GP practice that offers fast access to appointments for out-patient consultation, on-site diagnostic imaging, and day-case surgical treatment. Situated at 12 Hope Street in Cambridge, just off Mill Road, our practice is within easy reach of the City Centre and offers free on-site parking for ease of access. Consultations, scans and treatments are charged for. We welcome insured patients and those who choose to self-pay for their treatment. We are accepted by all major insurers. We aim to create a unique, quality clinical environment, with our patients at the heart of our service, and our teams of specialists on-hand to help you get the best from your private healthcare experience. Our aim is to provide first-class independent healthcare for the local community in a safe, comfortable and welcoming environment; one in which we would be happy to treat our own families.
